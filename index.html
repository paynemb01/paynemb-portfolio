This game has a few mechanics in it that took a bit of a struggle to implement, such as complex immunity frames, pathfinding, random spawning, avoiding pop-in, and generally making the game look nice despite it's simple appearance.

Employee Database

For this project I was working in a group of 4 where we had to break down a large amount of employee data, with over 1000 entries and place it into a MySql database.

We were given an excel spreadhseet that was the output of a survey completed by many employees at a company. From normalizing the large amount of data, then creating the migration functions that formatted the data, and then considering data integrity and security, we each took on different aspects to optimize this data set.

Finally we created a presentation and presented our process to the professor in a short powerpoint presentation.
